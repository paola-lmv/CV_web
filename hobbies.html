My love for nature drives my desire to explore, learn, and advocate for sustainable living and environmental stewardship.

Riding My Passion

Riding is my biggest passion because it enables me to create a unique bond with the horses. This special connection brings me immense joy and fulfillment in my life. I have been riding since I was three years old and have always been part of the same equestrian center.
